Our Rafting Trips

Family Float

Join our 2-hour Family Float, perfect for beginners and families with kids aged 5+. This trip features gentle Class I-II rapids, scenic river views, and plenty of fun for all. Our expert guides ensure a safe and enjoyable experience, with opportunities to spot local wildlife.

Adrenaline Rush

For thrill-seekers aged 16+, the 4-hour Adrenaline Rush tackles Class III-IV rapids. Test your skills on challenging waters with our experienced guides. This heart-pounding adventure offers breathtaking scenery and an unforgettable rush, perfect for seasoned rafters.

Overnight Escape

Embark on a 2-day Overnight Escape for ages 12+. This intermediate trip combines Class II-III rapids with serene floats, plus a night of camping under the stars. All meals and camping gear are provided, making it a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation.
